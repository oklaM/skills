## What is this?

This is a **battle-tested open source launch playbook** for developers, startups, and teams looking to maximize their GitHub Stars and build thriving developer communities. Based on real case studies and proven SOPs from successful open source launches.

**Key topics covered:**
- ⭐ GitHub Star growth strategies (500+ on launch day)
- 🎯 KOL collaboration with detailed pricing ($70-$1,500)
- 📱 Reddit marketing (15+ target Subreddits)
- 💬 1000+ developer groups for distribution
- 🤖 AI automation (Phantombuster, Opencord.ai)
- 📊 Data tracking and optimization

| Metric | Target |
|:---|:---|

| **First Week Stars** | 1,000+ |
| **Daily New Stars** | ≥100 (T+1 to T+7) |

| **Reddit Posts** | 3-5 Subreddits per day |
| **Group Distribution** | 50-100 groups per day |

## 💡 Core Philosophy

> The essence of open source: ask developers to contribute influence, ask B2B customers to contribute money 💰. Through SOP operations, an open source launch can generate hundreds to thousands of stars and bring many SMB leads. **Prepare your business model before open sourcing, so you can capture the traffic.**
